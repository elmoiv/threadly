Description

Threadly is a smart video-downloader for Threads.

Features:

  • Download Videos with sound.
  • Select from different qualities. (from 240p to 1080p)
  • Share downloaded videos to different apps.
  • Play videos within the app.
  • Powerful options to select from.

How to use:

  • Click on share button while viewing your Threads video.

    Or:

  • Copy Threads video URL and paste it in Threadly.
